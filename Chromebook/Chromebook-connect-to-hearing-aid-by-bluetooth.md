# Chromebook connect to hearning aid by Bluetooth

## ISSUE

A child just received a hearing aid in one ear and using the headphones is uncomfortable in the ear with the hearing aid.

The hearing aid can be connected by bluetooth to the Chromebook.

## RESOLUTION

To connect the hearing aid and the Chromebook, follow these steps.

1. Click on the clock in the lower right.
2. Click on `Settings`.
3. Click on `Bluetooth`
4. If Bluetooth is not on, click the button on the right to turn it on.
5. Click on the `Pair new device` button. A dialog will display all the devices that have their bluetooth on.
6. Click on the device to be paired.
