# Chromebook How to Update

## RESPONSE

How to Update Your Chromebook

Connect to Wi-Fi.
Make sure your Chromebook is online.

Open Settings.
Click the time at the bottom-right → click the gear icon.

Go to “About ChromeOS.”
In the left sidebar, scroll down and select About ChromeOS.

Check for updates.
Click Check for updates.

If an update is available, it will begin downloading automatically.

If your Chromebook is up to date, it will tell you.

Restart to apply the update.
After the update downloads, click Restart (or Restart to update) to finish.
