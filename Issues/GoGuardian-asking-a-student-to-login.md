# GoGuardian asking a student to login; should not happen

## ISSUE

Somehow, I student was being asked by GoGuardian to log in but the student should never be required to log into GoGuardian. It should be only by a teacher. Without GoGuardian being logged in, the student could not get to the assignments.

## RESOLUTION

Shut the laptop down completely. I restart is not sufficient.

When the laptop is started up, the issues with GoGuardian should be resolved and the student should be able to get to the assignment without dealing with GoGuardian.
